World-Class Facilities

Our state-of-the-art infrastructure and modern amenities create an inspiring learning environment that supports academic excellence and personal growth.

Huge Playground

Expansive sports grounds with football field, basketball court, and athletics track for comprehensive physical development and team sports.

Computer Lab

High-tech computer laboratory equipped with latest systems, high-speed internet, and modern software for digital literacy and programming skills.

Transport System

Safe and reliable school bus service covering extensive routes across Gonda with GPS tracking and trained drivers for student safety.

Smart Classrooms

Air-conditioned classrooms with smart boards, projectors, and interactive learning technology for engaging and effective education delivery.

Digital Library

Comprehensive collection of books, magazines, and digital resources with quiet study areas and research facilities for enhanced learning.

Science Labs

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories with modern instruments and safety measures for hands-on scientific learning.
